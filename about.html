About Me

Bio

I am Agboola Emmanuel, a Civil Engineer and Frontend Developer with a passion for sustainable construction, project management, and web technologies. My focus lies in integrating technology and engineering to build efficient, user-centered, and environmentally responsible systems.

Goals in This Program

My goal in the HNG 13 Internship is to refine my technical and collaborative skills through real-world challenges, teamwork, and mentorship. I aim to:

  • Improve my problem-solving and code optimization abilities.
  • Master practical frontend workflows using modern frameworks like React and Next.js.
  • Contribute meaningfully to team projects while learning agile collaboration.
  • Build confidence in scalable UI/UX implementation for real clients.
